The Perfect Steak Recipe

Juicy. Tender. Flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 1 Ribeye steak (1-1.5 inches thick)
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • Fresh rosemary & thyme
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Let steak rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over high heat.
  4. Sear the steak for 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Add butter, garlic, and herbs. Baste for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Remove and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
